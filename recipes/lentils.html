Lentils

Description

An all-seasons, delicious and traditional dish from the Mediterranean Cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Salt & Pepper

How to cook it:

  1. Rinse the lentils under lukewarm water to clean them up.
  2. Boil them over high heat in a pot for 15 minutes to soften and remove waste.
  3. While the lentils are boiling, mince the onion and cut the garlic into small cubes.
  4. After the 15 minutes have passed, remove the water and throw in the onion, the garlic, some olive oil and the tomato paste.
  5. Add water until the lentils are completely covered up.
  6. Boil in medium-low heat for another 45 - 60 minutes.
